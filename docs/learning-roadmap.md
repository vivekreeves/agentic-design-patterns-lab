# Learning Roadmap

## Goal
Build practical, production-minded understanding of agentic AI patterns from first principles to operations and FinOps.

## Sequence
1. Level 01: Agent foundations and primitive control flows
2. Level 02: Memory and adaptation mechanics
3. Level 03: Reliability and knowledge grounding
4. Level 04: Production orchestration and governance
5. Level 05: Advanced engineering patterns
6. Level 06: Operations, observability, and FinOps

## Recommended Cadence
- 3 lessons per week
- 1 implementation-focused review day
- 1 consolidation day for flash notes and mini-projects

## Mastery Signals
- You can choose the right pattern for a task and explain trade-offs.
- You can instrument quality, latency, and cost.
- You can design safe fallback paths for failures.
